About Me

I am a second year student at Georgia Tech, studying industrial engineering and minoring in computer science. I research and analyze twitter datasets for the School of Interactive Computing's Social Computing lab. I also coordinate consulting projects between nonprofits and teams of students for GT SCNO. My academic interests include statistics, data visualizations, and web development. Outside of school, I enjoy listening to music, watching Game of Thrones, and making espresso.
